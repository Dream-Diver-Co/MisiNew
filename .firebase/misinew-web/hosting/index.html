    OUR CARE OFFER

    We are a diagnostics and treatment center (BGGZ / SGGZ) in Rotterdam and offer psychological assistance for various complaints. You can contact various types of (neuro) psychologists at short notice, we work without an intake and treatment waiting list. We do everything we can to put you back in your power quickly.


    At MiSi NeuroPsy we are aware of the damage (physical/mental) that COVID-19 (the coronavirus) can cause. Despite the relaxations, we adhere to the following basic rules:


    - Wash + disinfect your hands

    - Cough and sneeze in your elbow

    - Stay at home if you have complaints and do a test immediately

    - Make sure there is enough fresh air


    By complying with the above rules, we hope not to spread the corona virus further.
