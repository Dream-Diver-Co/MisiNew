    Referrers

    Referrers

    MiSi NeuroPsy provides care within the BGGZ/SGGZ (18+) in the center of Rotterdam (walking distance from the central station). We offer insured care for the following diagnosis groups: 

    • Mood Disorders (Depression/Anxiety)
    • Personality
    • Somatoform
    • Dissociative
    • Neurocognitive
    • Developmental Disorders (Autism/ADHD)

    Excludes: Bipolar images, manifest psychotic disorders, active suicidality and/or self-mutilation, and severe neuropsychiatric images. Feel free to consult via 06-8143 7178 if you have any doubts about a particular reference. 

    If desired, we also offer treatment in which the patient is central. Attention is paid to the diversity in the patient's background (linguistic, psychosocial and cultural background).

    NB: Patients must take into account the deductible (€385 in 2023). 

    A valid referral is required for insured care. We prefer to receive referrals via healthcare domain. The referral letter must contain the following information:

    1. Referral date
    2. Referrer details: Name, position, address details, AGB code and signature.
    3. Patient details: Name, address details, date of birth, citizen service number (or registration number).
    4. Reason for referral: Suspicion of a DSM-5 designated disorder.
    5. Reference is made to General Basic/Specialist Mental Health Care.
